# Invitation-Only Admission Criteria

## Objective
Protect brand prestige and conversion quality by admitting only high-fit agencies/agents.

## Admission Funnel
1. Application submitted
2. Verification and compliance checks
3. Portfolio and brand quality scoring
4. Regional capacity review (scarcity caps)
5. Approval committee decision
6. Onboarding and activation

## Mandatory Requirements
- Valid licensing in operating market
- Verifiable legal entity and beneficial ownership info
- Demonstrated luxury inventory experience
- SLA readiness for international inquiries

## Quality Scoring (Example)
- Brand/portfolio quality: 35%
- Listing media and presentation quality: 25%
- Response operations and client service readiness: 20%
- Market fit and strategic value: 20%

## Regional Cap Rules
- Fixed max agencies per city/region
- Optional max share per single parent organization
- Category balancing to avoid over-concentration

## Re-qualification Policy
- Annual quality and performance review
- Rejection/suspension triggers:
  - Repeated service SLA breaches
  - Misrepresentation or compliance violations
  - Persistent low-quality content

## Waitlist Management
- Rank by score + strategic market fit
- Time-bound admissions windows
- Expiration rules for inactive approvals
