# Pricing Tier Matrix (Draft)

## Pricing Overview

| Tier | Annual Price (USD) | Ideal Customer | Positioning |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Private | 5,000–8,000 | Boutique luxury agencies entering global exposure | Premium baseline access |
| Elite | 15,000–30,000 | Established agencies seeking guaranteed premium visibility | Prestige + performance |

## Entitlements

| Capability | Private | Elite |
|---|---|---|
| Verified agency profile | Yes | Yes |
| Listing cap | Market-defined base cap | Higher cap (market-defined) |
| Standard directory exposure | Yes | Yes |
| Homepage spotlight eligibility | Limited / add-on | Priority / included windows |
| Editorial feature eligibility | Add-on | Included allocation |
| International newsletter placement | Add-on | Included allocation |
| Social showcase campaign | Add-on | Included allocation |
| Lead routing priority | Standard | Priority |
| Analytics | Basic | Advanced |
| Account management | Shared support | Dedicated manager |
| QBR/performance strategy | No | Yes |

## Add-On Packages

| Add-On | Unit | Suggested Price Logic |
|---|---|---|
| Homepage Spotlight | Per slot / per week | Premium fixed rate by market demand |
| Newsletter Feature | Per placement | Flat premium by audience segment |
| Social Showcase | Per campaign | Flat campaign fee + optional creative support |

## Scarcity & Inventory Policy
- Maximum participating agencies per market
- Maximum premium slots per month
- Waitlist activation once cap is reached
- Publish inventory windows quarterly for transparency

## Commercial Guardrails
- Minimum term: 12 months
- Default billing: annual prepay
- Discount policy: tightly controlled, exception-based
- No permanent discounting on premium inventory

## Recommended Launch Offers (Optional)
- Founding allocation: first N agencies per region at lower bound of tier range
- Annual prepay bonus: one additional newsletter placement
- Multi-market bundle: limited-time uplift package

## Sales Qualification Checklist
- Licensed and verified operating entity
- Luxury portfolio quality and photography standards
- Regional fit and inventory strategy
- Capacity to handle international buyer inquiries

## KPI Targets (Initial)
- Private → Elite upgrade rate
- Renewal rate by tier
- ARPA by region
- Premium slot sell-through
- Lead quality score and close-rate contribution
