# Commercial Policy (Premium Model)

## Purpose
Define the operating rules for a premium, scarcity-led marketplace targeting international luxury demand and recurring agency revenue.

## Revenue Model
- Core revenue: Annual agency memberships
- Secondary revenue: Premium exposure packages (homepage, newsletter, social showcases)
- Access policy: Invitation-only agencies/agents (no open self-serve onboarding)

## Membership Tiers

### Private Tier
- Price: USD 5,000–8,000 per year
- Listing allowance: up to market-defined cap
- Visibility: standard premium directory exposure
- Includes:
  - Verified agency profile
  - International listing eligibility
  - Basic lead dashboard
  - Standard support SLA

### Elite Tier
- Price: USD 15,000–30,000 per year
- Listing allowance: higher cap than Private
- Visibility: guaranteed premium placement windows
- Includes:
  - Homepage rotation eligibility
  - Editorial feature eligibility
  - Priority lead routing
  - Dedicated account manager
  - Quarterly performance review

## Exposure Packages (Add-ons)
- Homepage Spotlight
  - Limited monthly inventory
  - Fixed premium pricing or controlled bid model
- International Newsletter Feature
  - Curated high-intent audience placement
- Social Media Showcase
  - Editorial-style, brand-safe campaign slot

## Scarcity Rules
- Cap agency count per city/region
- Cap premium placement inventory per month
- Cap category concentration by segment where needed
- Enforce waitlist if market caps are reached

## Admission & Approval
- Manual approval required for all agencies and agents
- Minimum quality bar:
  - Verified legal entity and licensing
  - Portfolio quality threshold
  - Brand/reputation fit
  - Service readiness for UHNW clients
- Rejection reasons include quality mismatch, oversaturated region, or compliance concerns

## Contracting Terms
- Default contract term: 12 months
- Billing: annual prepay by default
- Optional installment plans at premium pricing
- Auto-renewal: yes, with defined notice window
- Setup/onboarding fee: optional for Elite and above

## Slot Allocation Rules
- Publicly document available premium inventory by market
- Reserve inventory for top-performing agencies to protect retention
- Prevent over-allocation that degrades premium value

## Compliance & Brand Protection
- Content/editorial standards apply to all listings and promotions
- Fraud and misrepresentation trigger suspension/removal
- Platform may revoke eligibility for persistent quality failures

## KPIs to Track Weekly
- Annual Contract Value (ACV)
- Renewal rate and expansion rate
- ARPA (average revenue per agency)
- Premium slot utilization
- Waitlist-to-conversion rate
- Lead quality-to-close performance by tier

## Governance
- Pricing/packaging review cadence: quarterly
- Market cap review cadence: monthly
- Executive sign-off required for:
  - New tier creation
  - Inventory cap changes
  - Discount policy exceptions
